# Release Safety

## TL;DR

This skill helps identify hidden rollout, rollback, and operational risks. It is especially useful when a change may affect release workflows or high-blast-radius repo surfaces.

## What I Do

- Flag hidden release implications
- Flag rollback hazards
- Push risky work toward explicit review
- Surface operational blast radius early

## When to Use Me

Use this skill whenever work may affect release, CI, deployment, or operational controls.
